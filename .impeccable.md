# Design Context — Shellack

## Target Audience
Solo developers, design engineers, vibecoders, and dev studios. People who build things fast, care about craft, and live in Slack. They appreciate good tooling and good design equally.

## Use Cases
- Discovering Shellack as a development automation tool
- Understanding what it does in 5 seconds
- Getting to the GitHub repo to self-host it

## Brand Personality
Playful meets editorial. The lobster is a character — friendly, unexpected, memorable. The tone is warm and confident but not corporate. Think Slack's approachability meets Anthropic's sophistication. Bold typography, generous whitespace, personality-driven. The product is serious but doesn't take itself too seriously.

## Visual Direction
- **Color palette:** Warm — clay/terracotta (#C17F4E) as accent, dark backgrounds, Slack purple hints, Anthropic sand tones
- **Typography:** Editorial display font + clean body. Big, bold headlines.
- **Layout:** Magazine-style sections with breathing room
- **Character:** The lobster (🦞) is a mascot, not just an emoji. It has personality.
- **Vibe:** "This tool was built by someone who cares about craft"
